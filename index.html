Overview

Heather Miller

Senior Technical Writer & Knowledge Management Specialist — I build documentation systems that stay accurate as products change weekly, not just documents that read well once.

I'm a Certified Professional Technical Communicator (CPTC) with 10+ years of experience writing and structuring documentation for complex software: multi-module ERP systems, SaaS platforms, and industrial equipment. My work sits at the intersection of content architecture and daily production reality — owning a knowledge base end to end, keeping it accurate through weekly releases, and building the structure (style guides, templates, DITA maps) that lets that pace be sustainable.

Recently I managed the entire knowledge management system for a SaaS ERP platform spanning 20+ modules on a weekly release cadence, administered end-to-end in ClickHelp. Earlier, at Epiroc Drilling Solutions, I worked in structured authoring — DITA/XML in XMetaL and Adobe FrameMaker — migrating legacy documentation into a modern component-content architecture.
